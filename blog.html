What is Depression?

Depression is a mental health condition that affects how you feel, think, and behave. It is a common and serious medical illness that negatively affects how you feel, think, and handle daily activities, such as sleeping, eating, or working. It can cause a lot of emotional pain and a variety of emotional and physical symptoms Sometimes the people who are depressed are not aware of it, and they may not even know that they are depressed.
