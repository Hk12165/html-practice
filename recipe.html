Pizza Recipe For a Veg Pizza

4 to 6 servings

Steps for making it.

  1. Activate the Yeast: In a bowl, combine 1-1.5 cups warm water, 1 tbsp sugar, and 2 tsp active dry or instant yeast. Let it sit for 5-7 minutes until it becomes frothy.
  2. Make the Dough: Add 3 to 4 cups all-purpose or bread flour, 2 tbsp olive oil, and 1 tsp salt. Mix until a sticky dough forms, then knead for 5-10 minutes until smooth and elastic.
  3. Let It Rise: Place the dough in an oiled bowl, cover it with a damp cloth or plastic wrap, and let it rest at room temperature for 1.5 to 2 hours, or until it doubles in size.
  4. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to a blazing hot 450° F (230° C). If you own a pizza stone or cast-iron skillet, leave it inside to heat up
  5. Shape the Dough: Punch down the risen dough and divide it in half (this makes two medium pizzas). On a floured surface, stretch or roll the dough into a 12-inch circle.
  6. Apply Sauce: Transfer the stretched dough to a greased or cornmeal-dusted pizza pan. Spread a thin layer of pizza sauce evenly, leaving a half-inch border for the crust.
  7. Add Cheese & Toppings: Sprinkle a generous layer of shredded mozzarella cheese, followed by your favorite toppings like pepperoni, mushrooms, or bell peppers. Add a final sprinkle of cheese to secure the toppings.
  8. Bake: Bake in the preheated oven for 10–15 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbling and the crust is golden-brown. Slice and serve hot

Ingredients:

  • Yeast
  • Water
  • Mozzarella cheese
